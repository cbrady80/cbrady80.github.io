Preston Recreation

By: Southeast Idaho High Country Tourism

Awaiting the fishermen and water-skiers who come from miles around are nine reservoirs. Peaceful campgrounds and refreshing hikes are within an easy distance. Snowmobiling is a favorite sport in the Bear River Range of the Wasatch Mountains and the Wellsville and Malad Ranges surrounding our town. The local hunting opportunities include: deer, elk, moose, pheasant, duck, geese and mountain lion. Hunting and fishing regulations and licenses are available at many of our stores.

Whether you are site-seeing or hunting the wildlife is abundant. Occasionally wildlife can be seen in our backyards and fields, but mostly in the beautiful mountains and near the many waterways. Historic sites and scenic views can also be navigated on the Pioneer Historic Byway that passes through Preston on Highway 91.

We hope that you will get off I-15 and come visit our beautiful hometown of Preston.
