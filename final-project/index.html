~ Welcome ~

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near select temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. We cater to temple workers, patrons, missionaries, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons.

Whatever services you plan to attend, the Temple Inn & Suites can accomodate you. In addition to our inviting hotel rooms for short term stays, we also have suites with a kitchenette for those who need a place for an extended stay and for full-time temple missionaries. We offer spaces for wedding receiptions or other family gatherings. We pride ourselves in offering comforting and helpful accomodations for all who wish to attend the House of the Lord.

Washington D.C. Temple

Standing on sprawling grounds about 10 miles north of the United States Capitol, the gold-spired Washington D.C. Temple is a well-known landmark along the Capital Beltway in Kensington, Maryland. The beauty of this soaring edifice is enhanced by a reflection pond near the visitors' center and a spouting water feature at the temple entrance. Sharing the 52-acre wooded site are the Washington D.C. Stake Center and the beautiful Washington D.C. Temple Visitors' Center, which hosts numerous interactive exhibits, a breathtaking reproduction of the Christus statue, and regular lectures and concerts throughout the year. Admission is free. At Christmastime, the grounds are set aglow during the Festival of Lights, which offers nightly concerts, a live nativity scene, and international nativity sets.
